Privacy Policy for TF4tChatBot

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TF4tChatBot ("the Extension") handles user data. We take your privacy seriously and ensure that the extension operates with transparency and minimal data access.

1. Information Collection and Use

TF4tChatBot is a productivity tool designed to enhance user experience on Free4talk. Regarding your data:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to any external servers owned by us.
  • API Keys: Your AI API keys (Google Gemini or Mistral) are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local). They are never shared with us or any third parties except the API providers you choose to use.
  • Chat History: The extension may temporarily store chat history in the browser's local memory to provide features like "History View" and "Summarization." This data remains on your local machine and is not uploaded anywhere.

2. Third-Party Services

When you use the AI features (Chat or Summarize), the necessary message content is sent directly to the AI provider you have configured:

  • Google Gemini API: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Mistral AI API: Subject to Mistral AI's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, the security of your data depends on your device's security. We recommend not sharing your API keys with anyone.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our official GitHub repository.
